Welcome to the Maze Game!

Objective: Navigate through a series of mazes and earn points by reaching the exits.

Game Rules:

  • You will play through 10 mazes in total.
  • Each maze has a time limit of 5 minutes.
  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate through the maze.
  • Each maze has two exits:
  • Reaching the first exit earns you 1 point.
  • Reaching the second exit earns you 3 points.
  • If you run out of time, you will earn 0 points for that maze.
